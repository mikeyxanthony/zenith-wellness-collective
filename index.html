Balance for real life

Holistic wellness and therapy that meets you where you are

I'm Elena Rossi, a licensed clinical psychologist specializing in holistic wellness, mindfulness-based therapy, and helping individuals create sustainable balance in their lives.

With over a decade of experience, I work with adults navigating life transitions, managing stress, and seeking deeper fulfillment. My approach integrates evidence-based psychology with mindfulness practices, creating a personalized path toward lasting well-being.

A holistic approach to wellness

My practice is built on the belief that true wellness extends beyond symptom management. I help clients develop sustainable practices that nurture mental, emotional, and physical well-being.

Whether you're navigating anxiety, seeking clarity during a life transition, or simply wanting to cultivate more presence and peace in your daily life, I provide a safe, non-judgmental space for exploration and growth.

I integrate cognitive-behavioral therapy, mindfulness-based stress reduction, and somatic practices to create a comprehensive, personalized treatment approach.
